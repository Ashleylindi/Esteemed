Meet Our Team

Iruka Umino

Instructor / Teacher

Iruka is a ninja instructor at Konoha Academy who teaches young ninjas. He plays a pivotal role in Naruto Uzumaki's early life, offering guidance and support.

Kakashi Hatake

Jonin / Team 7 Leader

Kakashi is a highly skilled ninja and the leader of Team 7, which includes Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura. He guides and mentors his students as they navigate their training and missions.

Tsunade Senju

Fifth Hokage / Medical Ninja

While primarily serving as the Hokage, Tsunade oversees the academy's operations and provides support to young ninjas. She is also an accomplished medical ninja.

Madara Uchiha

Legendary Shinobi / Antagonist

Madara is one of the most powerful ninjas in the series' history. He represents the dark side of ninja ideology and serves as a significant figure influencing the events that affect the academy and its students.
